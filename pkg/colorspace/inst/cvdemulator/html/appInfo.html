App Information

The R colorspace package, beside a variety of useful and user-friendly methods and functions to create effective color palettes, provides methods to emulate color vision deficiency (CVD) for different types of visual disorders.

This CVD emulator allows everyone to emulate the different deficiencies with different strengths on raster images (PNG or JPG/JPEG) to identify possible problems with existing plots and color maps, or to check whether or not new color palettes work for people with a specific color vision deficiency (e.g., the common red-green blindness).

Authors

Claus O. Wilke, Reto Stauffer, and Achim Zeileis.
